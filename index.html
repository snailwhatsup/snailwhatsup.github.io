九阳神功之三：布里渊理论预研1

Title@ Brillouin light scattering from surface acoustic waves in a subwavelength-diameter optical fibre
Author@ Jean-Charles Beugnot,…,Vincent laude&Thibaut Sylvestre
Journal@ Nature Communications
Abstract@ Brillouin Scattering in optical fibres is a fundamental interaction between light and sound with important implications ranging from optical sensors to slow and fast light. In usual optical fibres, light both excites and feels shear and longitudinal bulk elastic waves, giving rise to forward-guided acoustic wave Brillouin scattering and backward-stimulated Brilluin scattering. In a subwavelength-diameter optical fibre, the situation changes dramatically, as we here report with the first experimental observation of Brillouin light scattering from surface acoustic waves. These Rayleigh-type surface waves travel the wire surface at a specific velocity of 3,400 ms^-1 and back scatter the light with a Doppler shift of about 6 GHz. As these acoustic resonances are sensitive to surface defects or features, surface acoustic wave Brillouin scattering opens new opportunities for various sensing applications, but also in other domains such as microwave photonics and nonlinear palsmonics.
Translation@ 布里渊散射是光纤中光声基本的相互作用,在传感到快慢光等应用中有很重要的影响。在普通光纤中，光激发或吸收剪切和体波，从而提升前向和背向的布里渊散射。在亚波长直径光纤中，情形有戏剧性的变化，本文首次观察到基于声表面波的布里渊散射。这些瑞利型表面波在光纤表面以3400ms^-1速度传递，背向散射光的多普勒频移为6GHz。由于这种声学谐振对表面缺陷和特性非常敏感，因此表面声波型布里渊散射为多种传感应用乃至微波光子学和非线性等离表面激元等其他领域开启了新的机遇。

The complex and intriguing dynamics of light and sound interactions in tiny optical waveguides have recently witnessed a renewed interest because of their experimental realization in emerging key areas of modern pyhsics. For instance, the micro and nanostructuring of photonic crystal fibres(PCFs) allows for a tight confinement of both photons and phonons, giving rise to new characteristics for Brillouin scattering fundamentally different from those of standard optical fibres. These include the generation of multiple high-frequency hybrid transverse and longitudinal acoustic waves (LAWs) trapped within the small core of microstructured optical fibres. Strong photon-phonon coupling has also recently been reported in optical microcavities and new concepts have been introduced such as cavity or surface optomechanics, Brillouin cooling, on-chip Brillouin scattering and microcavity lasers. Moreover, the recent demonstration of simultaneous photonic and phononic bandgaps in nanostructured materials has led to the development of innovative opto-acoustic devices called phoxonic crystals. In yet another example, tailorable Brillouin scattering was recently reported in nano scal silicon waveguides.
声光在微小波导中复杂有趣的相互作用最近再次引发关注，主要得益于他们在现代物理关键领域的实验中被成功实现。举例来说，微纳结构的光子晶体光纤可以允许对光子和声子的紧束缚，从而使得其中布里渊散射的基本特性区别于标准光纤。包括小芯微结构光纤中多种较高频率横向和纵向混合声波的约束。最近光学微腔中声光强耦合现象也得到报道，同时引入了新的概念，入腔光机械或表面光机械、布里渊冷却、片上布里渊散射和微腔激光器。另外，最新的研究展示了微结构材料中可以同时实现声学带隙和光学带隙，从而导致名为phoxonic crystals(X学晶体)的新型声光器件的发展。在另一个例子中，报道了纳米尺度硅波导中可裁剪布里渊散射现象。

Among other microdevices, photonic silica microwires are the tiny as-yet underutilized cousins of optical fibers. These hair-like slivers of silica glass, fabricated by tapering optical fibres, enable enhanced nonlinear optical effects and applications not currently possible with comparatively bulky optical fibres. Kerr effect and stimulated Raman scattering for supercontinuum generation, Brillouin scattering(BS) in these tiny waveguides has not been explored yet. Until now, only transverse acoustic resonance have been reported in long optical fibre tapers with a large diameter of a few micrometres.
在其他的微器件中，光纤微米线是光纤微小但尚未得到充分利用的表亲。这些类似银色头发一样的二氧化硅玻璃线，通过拉锥普通光纤制备，可以提高光学非线性效应和相比较起来体块较大的普通光纤中所不能实现的应用，克尔效应和受激拉曼散射可以用于激发超连续谱，而微波导中布里渊散射尚未进行探索。到目前为止，只有长拉锥中的横向声学腔有过报道，其直径有数个微米之大。

In this work, we present what is to our knowledge the first complete measurement and numerical modelling of Brillouin scattering in a subwavelength-diameter optical fibres, revealing th full elastic wave distribution of such tiny optical wveguides. More specifically, we demonstrate the all-optical generation of a new class of surface acoustic waves(SAWs) and report the observation of SAW Brillouin scattering (SAWBS) in the bakward direction. In addition to surface waves, our experimental and theoretical investigations also show that silica microwires also exhibit several widely spaced Brillouin frequencies involving hybird shear and LAWs, as previously demonstrated in small-core PCFs. Our experimental measurements are checked against numerical simulations of the equations of elastodynamics including the electrostrictive stress.
本工作中，我们首次展示了亚微米光纤中布里渊散射的测量以及数值建模，揭示了这种微波导中完整的弹性波分布情况。特别是，我们演示了全光激发的新型表面声波并报道表面声波的反向布里渊散射。除了表面波之外，我们的实验和理论研究同时解释了二氧化硅微米线也表现出一些宽间距的布里渊频率，涉及到剪切和表面波的混合，这在小芯的光子晶体光纤中有报道。我们的实验结果利用弹性动力学和电致伸缩力的公式加以数值模拟验证。

Results
Principle. Let us first describe the opto-mechanical interaction under consideration. When coherent laser light is coupled and guided into a long uniform optical microwire as that shown schematically in Fig. 1, the light both excites and feels several types of elastic waves with similar micrometre-scale wavelengths. In standard optical fibres, the light is guided into the core and is therefore solely sensitive to both shear and longitudinal bulk acoustic waves, leading to well-known physical effects such as guided scoustic wave Brillouin scattering and stimulatd Brillouin scattering (SBS), respectively. In contrast, in subwavelength-diameter optical fibres, the guided light and particularl the evanescent field interact with the outr surface. Light can thus shake the wire and generate SAWs. The associated effective refractive index along the optical microwire. When passing through this moving refractive index grating, light undergoes Bragg scattering in the backward direction according to the phase-matching condition, as in fibre-based Brillouin scattering form LAWs. The backscattered Brillouin signal also undergoes a slight shift of its carrier frequency due to the Doppler effect according to the photon-phonon energy conservation law vB=2n_eff_V/lammda, with neff, the effective refractive index of the microwire; lammda, the optical wavelength in vacuum and V, the acoustic phase velocity. The acoustic velocity however significantly differs for surface, shear and longitudinal waves. Surface waves travel at a velocity between 0.87 and 0.95 of a shear wave (for fused silica, V_s=3,400ms^-1). This gives rise to new optical sidebands down-shifted from 6GHz in the light spectrum(FIG. 1c). On top of surface waves, there are also bulk hybrid acoustic waves (HAW) involving both shear and longitudinal components because of the mehanical boundary conditions(Fig. 1b). HAWs propagate at an intermediate speed between shear and longitudinal waves with acoustic frequencied ~9GHz. Furthermore, the sound energy density of surface waves is confined at the air-silica interface of the microwire, leading to small mechanical ripples of a few picometre, whereas, the HAW energy density remains trapped within the core without altering the wire shape(Fig. 1c).
结果
原理。让我们首先考虑描述光-机相互作用。如图1所示，当相干激光耦合并导入长的均匀光纤微米线中，激光同时激发并感受几种不同的弹性波，其波长在几微米左右，同激光波长大小类似。在标准光纤中，激光被导入芯层，因此只对剪切和纵向体波敏感，从而导致常见的物理效应如声学导波布里渊散射和受激布里渊散射。相反，在亚波长直径微光纤中，导光和倏逝波同光纤外表面相互作用。因此光可以振动波导从而产生表面波。与之相联系的机械波纹导致光纤微米线上有效折射率微小的周期性变化。当激光通过这些移动的折射率光栅，根据相位匹配条件，就会产生反向的布里渊散射，这一过程同纵波布里渊散射类似。反向散射的布里渊信号的载波频率会有一个多普勒平移，这是由于光子-声子的能量守恒规律所决定：VB=2n_eff_V/lammda，其中neff是微光纤的有效折射率，lammda是光真空波长，V是声波相速度。表面波剪切波和纵波的声速有显著的不同。表面波的传播速度大概是剪切波的0.87到0.95倍(对于熔融石英，V_s=3,4000ms^-1)。这就会引起光学频带6GHz的下降，图1c显示了波长移动图。在表面波频率之上，还会存在混合体声波(HAW)，包括剪切和纵波成分，这主要是由于机械边界条件决定，见图1b。混合波的传播速度为剪切波和纵波中间值，其声波频率为~9GHz。另外，表面声波的能量密度限制在空气和二氧化硅的界面出，使得机械波纹大概皮米量级，而体波的能量密度限制在芯层从而不会改变微光纤的形态。

Experimental setup. In our experiment, we investigated several subwavelength-diameter silica optial fibers, as those shown schematically in Fig.2a and in the image of Fig.2c. They were drawn from a commercial single-mode fibre(SMF) suing the heat-brush technique (for detail, see Fabrication method). They have a waist diameter ~1 um, a length of 8 cm and the input/output tapered fibre sections are 15-mm long. Figure 2b shows the experimenttal setup to detect Brillouin backscattering. As a pump laser, we used a narrow-linewidth continuous-wave distributed-feedback laser running at a wavelength of 1,550 nm. The laser output was split into two beams using a fibre coupler. One beam was amplified and injected in the optical microwire through an optical circulator, while the other beam served for detection. We then implemented a heterodyne detection in which the backscattered light from the microwire was mixed with the input coming from a second fibre coupler. The resulting beat note was then detected using a fast photodiode and averaged Brillouin spectra recorded using an electrical spectrum analyser.
实验设置。在我们的实验中，我们研究了几种不同亚波长直径的二氧化硅光纤，图2a为结构示意图，图2c为显微镜照片。他们是通过扫火法热锥商业单模光纤制备获得(细节请看制备方法一节)。其腰区直径为1微米，长度为8cm，并且输入输出的拉锥部分为15mm长。图2b展示了探测布里渊反向散射的实验设置。泵浦激光，我们使用了窄线宽的分布反馈连续光源，其工作波长为1550nm。激光输出利用耦合器分成两束，一路放大后经过环路器接入微光纤，另一路用来探测。接下来我们实现了外差探测，其中来自微光纤的反向散射光经过第二个耦合器同第二路的输入混合在一起。其产生的拍频记录利用高速光电探测器加以探测，平均后的布里渊谱信号利用电学频谱分析仪加以记录。

Experimental results. Figure 3a shows the experimental Brillouin spectrum for an input power of 100 mW and for a wire diametr of 1um. We see the clear emergence of several frequency peaks with different weights and linewidths in a raido-frequency range from 6 to 11 GHz. First ,the high frequency at 10.86 GHz originates from standard Brillouin scattering in the 2-m-long untapered fibre section and can be disregarded. More importantly, three other peaks appear at 8.33, 9.33 GHz, and slightly >10GHz, respectively. The two first peaks exhibit a linewidth ~25 MHz, in good agreement with the acoustic amplitude lifetime in fused silica (~10 ns). On the basis of numerical simulations described below, we identified them as resulting from Brillouin scattering from hybrid waves whereas the two other resonances ~6 GHz are clearly the signature of SAWs.
实验结果。图3a 显示了实验获得的布里渊频谱，其输入功率为100mW，微光纤腰区直径为1um。我们可以清楚的看到出现了多个频率峰值，其强度和线宽各有不同，频率分布从6到11GHz。首先，大概在10.86GHz的高频信号来源于2m长未拉锥的光纤产生的标准布里渊散射,因此可以不考虑。更重要的部分是其他三个峰，峰位分别为8.33GHz， 9.33GHz和稍微>10GHz。前两个峰的线宽~25MHz，同熔融石英的声波寿命十分吻合(~10ns)。基于下面数值模拟的结果，我们确定他们就是混合体波散射，而6GHz左右的谐振峰很明显是表面波产生。

Numberical simulations. To better understand the nature of these acoustic modes, we performed numerical simulations on the basis of the equatio of elastodynamics extended to account for electrostriction, a key physical effect in Brillouin scattering whereby matter become compressed under the effect of an electric field (for details, see Numerical method section). With respect to standard three-wave mixing SBS theory, this new modelling provides an excellent estimate of the theoretical Brillouin gain spectrum by computing the energy density of acoustic phonos generated by light. We considered the silica microwire as a rod-type cylinder and we took into account all elastic and optical parameters of silica. For the sake of simplicity, we neglected the two conical tapered sections of the fibe taper. We must also stress that we did not include in our model the radiation pressure induced by light at the surface of the microwire due to Maxwell stress. Using the method described in ref. 29, we have indeed theoretically estimated that the radiation pressure is <1% of the electrostrictive stress in silica wire of 1 um diameter. Radiation pressure should, however be taken into account for nanowires whose diameter is <500 nm. The results of our numerical simulations are shown in Fig. 3b-h. The slastic energy is plotted in Fig. 3b versus acoustic frequency and for a wire diameter varying from 1 to 1.35 um. As can be seen, we retrieve most of the surface and hybrid acoustic resonances, as those observed experimentally in Fig. 3a. There are, however, slight differences with experiment regarding the precise acoustic resonant grequencies. We attribute them to both the microwire uniformity and diameter uncertainty. Nevertheless, we can clearly identify two surface acoustic modes ~6 GHz, and three bybrid acoustic modes ~9 GHz. More information is provided by the modal distribution of the interacting waves. Figure 3c shows the spatial distribution of the optical mode intensity in the waist region at a wavelength of 1,550 nm. The black circle marks out the interface between silica and air. The optical mode is guided in the microwire with a rather long evanescent tail extnding outside silica. The tight confinement of the optical field into the microwire induces an electrostrictive stress ~30 times lagr than in usual optical fibre (up to 7 kPa as compared with 250 Pa under same incident optical power). As a comparison, the raidation pressure is only 50 Pa at the surface of microwire. The electrostrictive stress distribution, shown in Fig. 3d , follows the optical mode distribution without extending out of the microwire. The sound energy density of the two SAWs at 5.382 GHz and 5.772 GHz are plotted in Fig. 3e,f for a waist of 1.05 um. As can be seen, the elastic energy density is mainly localized below the wire surface. In contrast, the kinetic energy density of HAWs at 8.37 and 9.235 GHz, shown in Fig. 3g, h , are mostly confined within the core of the taper. The role of confining elastic energy is due to the elastic waveguide properties of such microwires, mostly its small diameter and hard mechanical boundary conditions.
数值模拟。为了更好的理解这些声学模式的特性，我们基于弹性动力学和电致伸缩公式进行数值模拟，电致伸缩是布里渊散射的关键,在电场作用下物质被压缩（细节参看数值方法一节）。根据标准三波混合SBS理论，这个新的模型为布里渊增益谱提供了很好的理论估算，其方法是计算光所激发的声子的能量密度。我们将微光纤当做棒状圆柱体并考虑二氧化硅所有的弹性和光学参数。我们忽略拉锥部分以作简化。同时，需要强调模型中没有考虑麦克斯韦应力在微光纤表面导致的辐射压。利用ref 29 中描述的方法，我们理论上估算出对于1um直径的微光纤该辐射压力小于电致伸缩力的1%。但是当纳米线直径<500nm时，就需要考虑辐射压力了。我们数值模拟的结果见图3b-h。弹性能对声学频率关系见图3b，其中微光纤直径从1um-1.35um。我们可以看出，我们检索出大部分的表面和混合声学谐振，同实验结果3a显示的一致。但是声学谐振频率的观测结果和理论结果有一些细微的不同。这主要是由于微光纤表面均匀度和直径的误差。尽管如此，我们还是可以清楚的分别出两个~6GHz的表面模式，和三个~9GHz混合声学模式。更多的信息可以从相互作用的波的模态分布获得。图3c给出了腰区波长1550nm的光的模式强度的空间分布。黑色圆标出二氧化硅和空气的界面。光学模式在微光纤中传导有很大部分泄露到二氧化硅外面。被强束缚到微光纤内的光场产生的电致伸缩比普通光纤中强30倍(同样强度的光激发，在微光纤中高达7kPa而普通光纤中大概250Pa)。作为对比，微光纤表面的辐射压只有50Pa。电致伸缩力的分布参见图3d，同束缚在微光纤中的模式分布一致。5.382GHz和5.772GHz两个表面波的声学能量密度见图3e,f，其微光纤直径为1.05um。我们可以看出来，弹性能量密度主要处于交界处。而与之相对比，8.37和9.235GHz处混合声波的动能密度见图3g,h，其主要局限在拉锥光纤的纤芯。微光纤的弹性波导特性是弹性能束缚的原因，一般指它的细小的直径和机械强度边界条件。

To go further into details, we plot in Fig.4a the full elastic wave spectrum over a wider range of wire diameter till 3.6 um. For eery diametr, the fundamental optical mode is recomputed and normalized . The refractive index neff increases smoothly with the diameter as the optical mode becomes more and more localized with silica rather than air. The two SAWs that we identified before exist for every diameter with a slight shift in frequency. Their acoustic energy is significant for small core but strongly reduces as the diameter increases beyond the optical wavelength(1.5 um). This observation is consistent with the simultaneous decrease of the oevrlap of SAW with the fundamental optical mode guided inside the core. Figure 4a also shows that, for a given microwire diameter, multiple HAW with widely spaced frequencied can be simultaneously excited, as in small-core PCFs. This means that in such small subwavelength waveguides, the light-sound interaction is fundamentally different from optical fibre. Brillouin spectrum is not simply the signature of a single bulk longitudinal sound wave. In such tiny waveguides, instead, waveguide boundary conditions induce a strong coupling of shear and longitudinal displacements, reulting in a much richer dynamics of light interaction with hybrid and SAWs. We can thus see the emergence of several avoided crossings and acoustic resonance splitting in Fig. 4a, due to the fact that HAWs are not orghogonal and thus stronglyinteract. This interaction is strong enough for certain acoustic frequencies to be forbidden at some aiti-crossing points, as shown by the white arrows in Fig. 4a. In contrast the two SAW branches cross without interacting for a diameter ~0.85 um because they have orthogonal polarizations. Figure 4b shows the associated transverse and axial displacements in the microwire for one of surface and hybrid modes, denoted A and B in fig. 4a. Transverse and axial displacements are simply defined as sqrt(ux^2+uy^2) and (|u_z|), respectively. R is defined as the ratio between spatial integrals of the shear and longitudinal dispalcements(R=1 for a pure shear wave). As can be seen, the surface acousic mod A at 5.382 GHz exhibits a transversal displacement of a few picometres and a weak axial displacement just below the wire surface. Clearlt, this is the signature of a surface Rayleigh wave that combines both a longitudinal and transverse motion to create mechanical ripples with an elliptic orbit motion. In contrast, the displacements for the hybrid mode B are smaller (R=0.589) and mainly localized within the core of the wire.
