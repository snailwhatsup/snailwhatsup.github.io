九阳神功之五：布里渊预研SAW数值模拟

SAW Gas Sensor

Introduction

A surface acoustic wave(SAW) is an acoustic wave propagating along the surface of a solid meterial.Its amplitude decays rapidly, often exponentially, with the depth of the material. SAWs are featured in many kinds of electronic components, including filters, oscillators, and sensors. SAW devices typically use electrodes on a piezoelectric material to convert an electric signal to a SAW, and back again.

In this model, you investigate the resonance frequencies of a SAW gas sensor. The sensor consists of an interdigitated transducer(IDT) etched onto a piezoelectric LiNbO3(lithium niobate) substrate and covered with a thin polyisobutylene(PIB) film. The mass of the PIB film increase as PIB selectively adsorbs CH_2_Cl2 (dichloromethane, DCM) from air. This causes ashift in resonance which slightly lowers the resonance frequency for the same SAW mode.

表面声波是在固体表面传播的声波。其强度随着深度指数衰减。表面声波可以用来实现很多电学器件，如滤波器、振荡器和传感器。声表面波器件通常在PZT材料表面制备电极以实现电信号和表面声波的互相转换。

在这个模型中，你主要研究表面声波气体传感器的谐振频率。传感器主要包括在LiNbO3基底的PZT，在PZT表面所腐蚀的叉指换能器（IDT）和上面覆盖的PIB薄膜。当PIB有选择的吸收空气中的CH2Cl2时，其薄膜质量会增加。这就会导致谐振腔的峰位稍微变小。

IDT used in SAW devices may have hundreds of identical electrodes, and each electrode can be about 100 times longer than it is wide. You can therefore neglect the edge effect and reduce the model geometry to the periodic unit celll shown in Figure 2. The height of this cell does does not have to extend all the way to the bottom of the substrate but only a few wavelength down, so that the SAW has almost died out at the lower boundary.

SAW器件中的IDT往往有数百个电极，每一个电极的长度大概有宽度的100倍以上。因此可以忽略边界效应，将模型简化为fig2中所示的周期单元。并且单元的高度也不需要扩展到实际底边，只需要波长的数倍，表面波在这个深度已经基本上衰减为0了。

Set up the model using the predefined Piezoelectric Devices multiphysics interface. In 2D, a Plane Strain assumption is used for the Solid Mechanics physics. Hence the out-of-plane strain component is zero. This should be a valid assumption, considering that the SAW is generated in the plane of the model and hence any variation in the out-of-plane direction can be cosidered minimal.

利用已经定义好的PZT器件多物理场界面加以设置。在2D模型中，固体物理采用平面应力假设。因此其法向应力分量为0。考虑到SAW在平面内产生，平面法向分量非常小，该假设基本符合实际情况。

Boundary Conditions

In order to define the model, you need to apply structural and electrical boundary conditions.

As one can assume that the surface wave dies off within two to three wavelengths from the surface, the lower boundary is fixed. This enforces a zero structural displacement but does not contribute to any significant reflection from the lower boundary back into the bulk of the substrate as long as we are observing surface waves and in particular Rayleigh waves.

为了定义模型，你需要加载结构和电学边界条件。

由于我们可以假设表面波在界面下两到三倍波长深度内就衰减为0,因此下边界条件就可以确定了。其结构位移为0，且在我们观察表面特别是瑞利波时不会有任何明显的反射贡献。

The electrodes have a much higher electrical conductivity compared to PIB and LiNbO3. Hence, one can expect each of the electrodes to be isopotential. This is why you do not need to model the domains that constitute the electrodes but can simply use appropriate boundary conditions on all the outer boundaries of each electrodes to indicate what type of isopotential state it is in. The boundaries of the left terminal is set to electrical ground, and those of the right terminal are assigned to a Floating Potential with zero surface charge accumulation. This combination of electrical boundary conditions corresponds to an open circuirt configuration, which is typically suitable for sensing applications.

电极的电导率比PIB和LiNbO3高的多。因此，我们可以认为每一个电极是等电势的。这就是为什么你不需要为电极域进行建模，而只需要为所有的电极使用合适的简化以表明其电势即可。其中左边的电极被设为接地，而右边的电极则为高电势，其中没有表面电荷累计。这种电学边界条件的组合同开放电路配置一致，后者适用于典型的传感应用。

Use periodic boundary conditions to dictate that the electric potential and displacements are the same along both vertical boundaries of the geometry. When using periodic boundary conditions,one need to ensure that the mesh on the ertical boundaries on the left of the unit cell and the vertical boundries on the right of the unit cell are identical. This is achieved by first creating the mesh on the vertical boundaries on the left and then using the Copy Edge feature to create the exact same mesh on the veritcal boundaries on the right.

使用周期边界条件来表明左右两边竖直方向的电势和位移都是一致的。尤其是需要保证划分网格的时候两边一致。这主要通过首先在左边竖直方向划分网格，然后使用Copy Edge的功能来在右边实现完全相同的边界条件。

All other boundaries are left to the default boundary conditions which are Free for Solid Mechanics and Zero Charge for Electrostatics, respectively.

所有其他的边界条件可以设为默认，其中固体边界条件为自由，而电学边界条件为0电荷。

ESTIMATING THE SAW FREQUENCY

The use of periodic boundary condition implies that the frequencies of interest will correspond to wavelengths that are integer fractions of the width of the geometry. The lowest SAW eigenmode has its wavelength equal to the width of the geometry, i.e. 4 um. Using this along with the Rayleigh wave velocity for the given piezoelectric substrate material, one can find an estimate of the resonance frequency of interest. The information can be used in the eigen frequency solver, which will help it to find out the resonance frequencies close to this estimated number. In this model, you use a YZ-cut LiNbO3 whose Rayleigh wave velocity(\nuR) is around 3488 m/s. This gives an estimate of the lowest SAW frequency(f0) to be 872 MHZ.

使用周期边界条件暗示了我们感兴趣的频率对应的表面波波长为几何宽度的(周期)整分数之一，这里最小的SAW本征模式波长为几何结构的宽度，4um。利用该波长结合PZT材料的瑞利散射声速，我们可以估算出谐振频率，这有利于我们寻找实际的谐振频率点。该模型中瑞利声速为3488 m/s。因此最低频率为872MHz。

Results and Discussion

The anti-resonance and resource frequencies evaluate to approximately 850 MHz and 855 MHz, respectively. Figure 3 and Figure 4 show the corresponding SAW modes. Figure 5 and Figure 6 show the electric potential distribution characteristics for these solutions.

Exposing the sensor to a 100 ppm concentration of DCM in air leads to a resonance frequency shift of approximately 200 Hz downwards. This is computed by evaluating the resonance frequency before and after adding the density of adsorbed DCM to that of the PIB domain.

Note that the computational mesh is identical in both these solution. This implies that the relative error of the frequency shift similar to that of the resonance frequency itself. Thus, the shift is accurately eveluated despite beint a few magnitudes smaller than the absolute error of the resonance frequency.

九阳神功之四：布里渊预研2

微光纤中的布里渊声波的研究，我们可以分解为以下几个部分：
1，光学部分，微光纤中的光学模式
2，声学部分，微光纤中的声学模式
3，耦合部分，微光纤中电致伸缩力

微光纤中的模式分布，其解析方法是典型的阶跃型光纤的模式计算法，基于亥姆霍兹方程计算矢量精确解，利用简并方法求解标量解。
常用的数值方法是FEM有限元法，一般基于comsol软件。

微光纤中的声学模式，其解析方法是基于声场方程，由于外激励和损耗的存在，因此比普通的亥姆霍兹方程复杂一些，但是基本结构还是类似的。
其常用的数值方法很多，FEM是很重要的一种。

我们首先看声学部分。

基于守恒和变化的基本规律，我们可以获得描述声学基本规律的方程为声场方程

声场方程决定了声学模式的根本特性，类似于麦克斯韦方程-波动方程对于光学模式。但是由于它们的普适特点，对于具体的器件，还需要针对特定条件加以变化，以方便分析。

针对具体的物质，需要列出本构方程

针对边界的情况，需要将声场方程针对边界的一般性结果加以单独列出，成为边界条件

当我们将以上三种条件一起考虑，便可以解决具体的问题。

声场方程的解可以解读分类为体波、剪切波和表面波三种形式。

由于声场方程是普适的，而具体的器件其区别在于边界条件和本构方程的不同，因此我们可以说声学模式是由器件本身的结构决定的。通过器件的结构我们可以获得其中可能存在的声学模式是什么。

激励的作用是馈入能量，能量的耦合是通过对声学结构/参数的影响实现。没有激励我们只能说这个声学结构中允许存在哪些模式，有了激励我们可以有选择性的使得某个模式成为现实。

理想的情况下激励只是提供了能量，实际上激励的某些耦合方式会对某些参数产生影响，进而改变声学模式，因此需要酌情考虑。

其中\rho是密度，一个标量。u是位移，一个矢量。c是弹性系数，一个张量。f是外力，一个矢量。

数学公式的威力，犹如源代码之于编程。数学公式一出，诸法皆退。

Labview有以下重要的概念

其中array是数据的常用组织方式, queue则是很重要的数据和任务同步工具, Queue可以用来实现堆栈的功能，因此queue被放在了同步的函数选板中。队列中数据分为前端后后端，前端为旧数据，后端是新数据。
常用的队列函数有五个：获取queue引用 Obtain queue，元素入队列enqueue element，元素出队列Dequeue element, 清空队列flush queue和释放队列引用release queue。其中获取队列引用是，有一个最大长度的选项，该选项如果为-1则队列会不断增长，需要手动协调速度，而设定最大长度后，队列满后新元素会之一等待直到有空位为止；在元素入队列的设定中，有一个选项叫做超时timeout,该选项对应于有最大队列长度，如果新元素在等待设定时间后仍然无法入队列，则结束本次等待，等待超时的新元素会被丢弃。元素出队列时需要注意，队列是先入先出FIFO，前端元素出队列之后，该元素会被删除。
另外还有两个操作函数：队列最前端插入元素Enqueue element at opposite end，有损耗元素入队列lossy enqueue element，前者是是先入后出功能FIFO，可以实现堆栈功能。后者是自动删除前端元素，在末端插入元素，可以实现缓冲区buffer功能(chart图便是缓冲区技术实现的)。
最后两个功能是查询功能，获取队列状态get queue status和预览队列元素preview queue element，预览的功能不会删除前端元素。

因此如果在两个循环之间同步数据和任务时，队列是非常好的工具，如同使用堆栈一样，内存回收非常方便，同时其运行效率远高于数组。

  1. 使用固定长度数组，预估所需数组长度，新建并初始化，在使用时只需要利用循环次数i对数组长度求余数作为索引以便循环replace即可。
  2. 利用数组实现队列功能。

九阳神功之三：布里渊理论预研1

Title@ Brillouin light scattering from surface acoustic waves in a subwavelength-diameter optical fibre
Author@ Jean-Charles Beugnot,…,Vincent laude&Thibaut Sylvestre
Journal@ Nature Communications
Abstract@ Brillouin Scattering in optical fibres is a fundamental interaction between light and sound with important implications ranging from optical sensors to slow and fast light. In usual optical fibres, light both excites and feels shear and longitudinal bulk elastic waves, giving rise to forward-guided acoustic wave Brillouin scattering and backward-stimulated Brilluin scattering. In a subwavelength-diameter optical fibre, the situation changes dramatically, as we here report with the first experimental observation of Brillouin light scattering from surface acoustic waves. These Rayleigh-type surface waves travel the wire surface at a specific velocity of 3,400 ms^-1 and back scatter the light with a Doppler shift of about 6 GHz. As these acoustic resonances are sensitive to surface defects or features, surface acoustic wave Brillouin scattering opens new opportunities for various sensing applications, but also in other domains such as microwave photonics and nonlinear palsmonics.

Principle. Let us first describe the opto-mechanical interaction under consideration. When coherent laser light is coupled and guided into a long uniform optical microwire as that shown schematically in Fig. 1, the light both excites and feels several types of elastic waves with similar micrometre-scale wavelengths. In standard optical fibres, the light is guided into the core and is therefore solely sensitive to both shear and longitudinal bulk acoustic waves, leading to well-known physical effects such as guided scoustic wave Brillouin scattering and stimulatd Brillouin scattering (SBS), respectively. In contrast, in subwavelength-diameter optical fibres, the guided light and particularl the evanescent field interact with the outr surface. Light can thus shake the wire and generate SAWs. The associated effective refractive index along the optical microwire. When passing through this moving refractive index grating, light undergoes Bragg scattering in the backward direction according to the phase-matching condition, as in fibre-based Brillouin scattering form LAWs. The backscattered Brillouin signal also undergoes a slight shift of its carrier frequency due to the Doppler effect according to the photon-phonon energy conservation law vB=2n_eff_V/lammda, with neff, the effective refractive index of the microwire; lammda, the optical wavelength in vacuum and V, the acoustic phase velocity. The acoustic velocity however significantly differs for surface, shear and longitudinal waves. Surface waves travel at a velocity between 0.87 and 0.95 of a shear wave (for fused silica, Vs=3,400ms^-1). This gives rise to new optical sidebands down-shifted from 6GHz in the light spectrum(FIG. 1c). On top of surface waves, there are also bulk hybrid acoustic waves (HAW) involving both shear and longitudinal components because of the mehanical boundary conditions(Fig. 1b). HAWs propagate at an intermediate speed between shear and longitudinal waves with acoustic frequencied ~9GHz. Furthermore, the sound energy density of surface waves is confined at the air-silica interface of the microwire, leading to small mechanical ripples of a few picometre, whereas, the HAW energy density remains trapped within the core without altering the wire shape(Fig. 1c).

原理。让我们首先考虑描述光-机相互作用。如图1所示，当相干激光耦合并导入长的均匀光纤微米线中，激光同时激发并感受几种不同的弹性波，其波长在几微米左右，同激光波长大小类似。在标准光纤中，激光被导入芯层，因此只对剪切和纵向体波敏感，从而导致常见的物理效应如声学导波布里渊散射和受激布里渊散射。相反，在亚波长直径微光纤中，导光和倏逝波同光纤外表面相互作用。因此光可以振动波导从而产生表面波。与之相联系的机械波纹导致光纤微米线上有效折射率微小的周期性变化。当激光通过这些移动的折射率光栅，根据相位匹配条件，就会产生反向的布里渊散射，这一过程同纵波布里渊散射类似。反向散射的布里渊信号的载波频率会有一个多普勒平移，这是由于光子-声子的能量守恒规律所决定：VB=2n_eff_V/lammda，其中neff是微光纤的有效折射率，lammda是光真空波长，V是声波相速度。表面波剪切波和纵波的声速有显著的不同。表面波的传播速度大概是剪切波的0.87到0.95倍(对于熔融石英，V_s=3,4000ms^-1)。这就会引起光学频带6GHz的下降，图1c显示了波长移动图。在表面波频率之上，还会存在混合体声波(HAW)，包括剪切和纵波成分，这主要是由于机械边界条件决定，见图1b。混合波的传播速度为剪切波和纵波中间值，其声波频率为~9GHz。另外，表面声波的能量密度限制在空气和二氧化硅的界面出，使得机械波纹大概皮米量级，而体波的能量密度限制在芯层从而不会改变微光纤的形态。

Numberical simulations. To better understand the nature of these acoustic modes, we performed numerical simulations on the basis of the equatio of elastodynamics extended to account for electrostriction, a key physical effect in Brillouin scattering whereby matter become compressed under the effect of an electric field (for details, see Numerical method section). With respect to standard three-wave mixing SBS theory, this new modelling provides an excellent estimate of the theoretical Brillouin gain spectrum by computing the energy density of acoustic phonos generated by light. We considered the silica microwire as a rod-type cylinder and we took into account all elastic and optical parameters of silica. For the sake of simplicity, we neglected the two conical tapered sections of the fibe taper. We must also stress that we did not include in our model the radiation pressure induced by light at the surface of the microwire due to Maxwell stress. Using the method described in ref. 29, we have indeed theoretically estimated that the radiation pressure is <1% of the electrostrictive stress in silica wire of 1 um diameter. Radiation pressure should, however be taken into account for nanowires whose diameter is <500 nm. The results of our numerical simulations are shown in Fig. 3b-h. Clearlt, this is the signature of a surface Rayleigh wave that combines both a longitudinal and transverse motion to create mechanical ripples with an elliptic orbit motion. In contrast, the displacements for the hybrid mode B are smaller (R=0.589) and mainly localized within the core of the wire.
为了进一步深入细节，我们在图4a中画出了弹性波频谱随着微光纤直径变化到3.6um的情况。对于每一个直径，光学基模都重新计算并且归一化。有效折射率neff随着直径增大而平滑增加 ，这是由于光学模式越来越集中于光纤内而非空气。之前确认的两个表面声波在每一个直径都存在，但是频率有轻微的移动。他们的声学能量在小直径时很显著，但是对于大于1.5um的直径有明显的下降。持续的观察可以随着光学基模向纤芯集中的同时两个表面波的交叠也在下降。图4a也展示了对于一个给定的微光纤直径，具有广泛频率分布的多种混合声学模式也可以同时被激发，这同小纤芯的光子晶体光纤情形一致。以上结果意味着，在如此小直径的亚波长波导中，光声相互作用是从本质上不用于标准光纤的。布里渊频谱不是简单的一个体块纵向声波。在如此小的波导中，相反，波导边界条件引发了剪切和纵向位移的强耦合，从而导致光同表面声波和混合体声波更加丰富的动态相互作用。我们因此可以在图4a中看到一些非交叉和谐振分裂现象，这是由于混合体波是非正交因此有很强的相互作用。这些相互作用足以使得某些反交叉点处特定的声学频率不会出现,在图4a中白色箭头可以看出。与之相反，在直径~0.85um处两个表面声波分支相互交叉而不会出现相互作用，这是由于它们是正交偏振。图4b显示了微光纤中一个表面和混合模式的相关联的横向和轴向位移，在图4a中标记为A和B。横向和轴向位移分别简单定义为sqrt(ux^2+uy^2)和(|uz|)。R定义为剪切和纵向位移的空间积分比率(对于纯剪切波R=1)。正如我们可以看到的，表面声波模式A(5.382GHz)表现出一个数皮米的横向位移，而仅在微米线界面下有很弱的轴向位移。很明显，这是表面瑞利波的特征，其结合了纵向和横向运动以产生了椭圆运动的机械波纹。与其相对比，混合模式B的位移较小(R=0.589)且主要局域在光纤纤芯内部。

Discussion
One may then wonder what is the scattering efficiency and if the threshold for stimulated regime is raised for SAWBS. To answer these important issues, we have estimated the Brillouin gain from the experimental spectrum in Fig. 3a. Starting from the usual theory of Brillouin scattering, the Brillouin gain gB can be expressed as gb=(4\pi neff^8 P12^2)/(c \rho \lambda^3 \nuB \Delta\nuB),where neff is the effective refractive index of the microwire, P12 is the elasto-optic constant and \rho is the mean density of used silica (SiO2), c is the speed of light in vacuum, \lambda is the optical wavelength, \nuB and \Delta\nuB are the SAW frequency shift and FWHM width of the spectrum. From measurements, we get \nuB=5.85GHz and \Delta\nuB=20 MHz for the first SAW peak, thus we found gB=1.410^-12 mW^-1, to be compared with gB=3.10^-11 mW^-1 for SMF fibres. If we now consider the effective mode aea Aeff to get the Brillouin gain factor by unit length, we found that gB/Aeff=8W^-1m^-1 for a 1-um-diameter silica wire whereas it is only 0.4 W^-1m^-1 for SMF fibers. That clearly means that the scattering efficiency is rather high. When examining carefully the spectrum of Fig. 3a, one can indeed clearly see that the SAW efficiency in the microwire is just 2-dB below that of Brillouin scattering in the 2 m-long fibre pigtails. However the threshold for stimulated regime is not raised over so short propagation distance. It can readily be derived from the Brillouin gain using the following standard formula Pth=(21Aeff)/(Kg_B_Leff), where Leff is the effective length that accounts for losses and K the polarization factor (K=1.5). Assuming 10dB of linear losses for the microwire from measurement, we found a high threshold power of Pth = 60 W in silica microwire which is well above the continuous-wave power used in the experiment (100 mW). This high threshold power level could, however, be achieved using amplified long nanosecond optical pulse instead of continuous-wave power. There also could be improvement in microwire parameters such as optical loss. In addition, we can expect that the surface Brillouin threshold can be substantially reduced in optical microwire made of ighly nonlinear chalcogenide glasses.
讨论：
我们接下来可以考虑散射效率和表面波的激发阈值是否上升。为了回答这些重要的问题，我们从实验光谱图3a估计了布里渊的增益。我们从普通的布里渊散射理论出发，布里渊增益gb可以表示为公式gB=(4\pineff^8P12^2)/(c\rho\lambda^3\nuB*\Delta\nuB),其中neff是微光纤的有效折射率，P12是弹光系数，而\rho是熔融石英(SiO2)的平均密度，c是光在真空中的速度，\lambda是光的波长，\nuB和\Delta\nuB是表面波频率移动以及光谱的半峰宽。通过测量，我们得到第一个表面波\nuB=5.85GHz，\Delta\nuB=20MHz，因此有gB=1.410^-12mW^-1，而标准单模光纤的gB=3.10^-11 mW^-1。如果我们现在考虑有效模式面积Aeff以获得单位长度布里渊增益因子，我们发现对于1um的微光纤有gB/Aeff=8W^-1m^-1，而对于普通单模光纤其值仅为0.4W^-1m^-1。这就清晰的表明散射效率更高。当仔细测量图3a中的光谱，我们确实可以清楚的看到表面波效率仅仅比2m长普通光纤尾纤的布里渊散射低2dB。然而激发阈值在如此短的传播距离上并没有上升。这很容易用以下公式从布里渊增益计算出来Pth=(21Aeff)/(Kg_B_Leff),其中Leff指考虑到损耗的有效长度，K是偏振因子(K=1.5)。我们假设微光纤的线性损耗为10dB，则微光纤会有很高的阈值功率Pth=60W，远高于实验中使用的连续激光功率(100mW)。不过，这么高的功率阈值可以通过使用放大长纳秒脉冲激光替代连续激光来获得。也可以改进微光纤参数如损耗。另外，我们可以预期表面布里渊阈值可以通过拉锥高非线性光纤如含硫光纤来从本质上加以解决。

Conclusion
In conclusion, we have reported a clear-cut evidence of a new type of Brillouin light scattering driven by SAWs by use of a subwavelength-diameter silica fibre. It has been further shown that the SAWs reported here travel the wire surface at a specific velocity of 3.400 ms^-1 and give rise to new useful optical sidebands around 6GHz in the scattering spectrum. This work thus may present a landmark to foster the investigate of such surface waves in many different photonic platforms, such as PCFs and integrated optical devices (for example, chalcogenide and silicon chips). In addition, SAWBs can find strong potential applications for optical sensing and detection because these acoustic waves are inherently sensitive to surface features and defects and are already used in many sensing systems (for example, MEMS). They could be seen as a new class of Brillouin optical sensors that rely on the modulation of SAWs to sense a physical phenomenon (for example, temperature, stress, gas). Changes in amplitude and frequency of the Brillouin signal through the silica microwire could be advantageously used to measure the presence of the desired phenomenon. One can also imagine a strong interaction between surface waves and surface plasmons by depositing a thin layer of gold or silver. These results thus show the potential of optical microwires and nanowires for surface Brillouin scattering, optical sensing and nonlinear plasmonics. Finally, this work contributes to the further understanding of the intriguing light-sound interactions in subwavelength optica and nanophotonics.
结论
综上而言，我们报道了基于亚波长直径微光纤声表面波的新型布里渊散射的明确证据。我们进一步揭示了此处报道的表面波在微光纤表面传导，其特定速度为3,400ms^-1，并且在散射频谱中得到了新的有价值的6GHz左右的光学边带。该工作是促进此类表面声波在不同光学体系中研究的里程碑，如光子晶体光纤和集成光学器件(如：含硫光纤和硅基芯片)。另外，声表面波在传感和探测领域有很强的应用前景，因为这类声波在本质上对表面特性非常敏感，同时已经被运用在很多传感体系中(如MEMS)。他们可以成为一种新型的布里渊光学传感器，利用对表面声波的调制来探测特定的物理现象(如温度、压力、气体等)。微光纤中布里渊信号的强度和频率可以成为探测这些物理现象的一大优势。另外还可以想象通过沉积一层很薄的金或银，可以获得很强的表面波和表面等离激元的相互作用。这些结果因此可以演示光纤微米线和纳米线在表面布里渊散射、光学传感和非线性等离激元的应用潜力。最后，本工作加深了对亚波长光学和纳米光学中有趣的光-声相互作用本质的理解。

Methods
Fabrication method. The silica microwire has been tapered from a standard telecom fibre(SMF-28) using the heat-brush technique. The fibre is softened on its cintral part with a small butane flame. The gas flow is monitored and regulated by a mass-flow controller. The flame is kept fixed while the two translation stages elongate the fibre to create the microwire. The microwire shape is fully controlled by the trajectories of the two translation stages. The untapered parts. upstream and downstream the microwire, allow a very easy light injection and collection. During the pulling, a laser light is coupled inside the microwire and collected at the output end to control several parameters(transmitted power, mode shapre and spectrum). With this home-made pulling platform, we routinely achieved light transmission larger than 90% over the full fibre including the tapered and untapered parts, even with microwire diameters of a few hundred nanometres and lengths up to several centimeters.
