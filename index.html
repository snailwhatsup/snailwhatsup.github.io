九阳神功之三：布里渊理论预研1

Title@ Brillouin light scattering from surface acoustic waves in a subwavelength-diameter optical fibre
Author@ Jean-Charles Beugnot,…,Vincent laude&Thibaut Sylvestre
Journal@ Nature Communications
Abstract@ Brillouin Scattering in optical fibres is a fundamental interaction between light and sound with important implications ranging from optical sensors to slow and fast light. In usual optical fibres, light both excites and feels shear and longitudinal bulk elastic waves, giving rise to forward-guided acoustic wave Brillouin scattering and backward-stimulated Brilluin scattering. In a subwavelength-diameter optical fibre, the situation changes dramatically, as we here report with the first experimental observation of Brillouin light scattering from surface acoustic waves. These Rayleigh-type surface waves travel the wire surface at a specific velocity of 3,400 ms^-1 and back scatter the light with a Doppler shift of about 6 GHz. As these acoustic resonances are sensitive to surface defects or features, surface acoustic wave Brillouin scattering opens new opportunities for various sensing applications, but also in other domains such as microwave photonics and nonlinear palsmonics.
Translation@ 布里渊散射是光纤中光声基本的相互作用,在传感到快慢光等应用中有很重要的影响。在普通光纤中，光激发或吸收剪切和体波，从而提升前向和背向的布里渊散射。在亚波长直径光纤中，情形有戏剧性的变化，本文首次观察到基于声表面波的布里渊散射。这些瑞利型表面波在光纤表面以3400ms^-1速度传递，背向散射光的多普勒频移为6GHz。由于这种声学谐振对表面缺陷和特性非常敏感，因此表面声波型布里渊散射为多种传感应用乃至微波光子学和非线性等离表面激元等其他领域开启了新的机遇。

The complex and intriguing dynamics of light and sound interactions in tiny optical waveguides have recently witnessed a renewed interest because of their experimental realization in emerging key areas of modern pyhsics. For instance, the micro and nanostructuring of photonic crystal fibres(PCFs) allows for a tight confinement of both photons and phonons, giving rise to new characteristics for Brillouin scattering fundamentally different from those of standard optical fibres. These include the generation of multiple high-frequency hybrid transverse and longitudinal acoustic waves (LAWs) trapped within the small core of microstructured optical fibres. Strong photon-phonon coupling has also recently been reported in optical microcavities and new concepts have been introduced such as cavity or surface optomechanics, Brillouin cooling, on-chip Brillouin scattering and microcavity lasers. Moreover, the recent demonstration of simultaneous photonic and phononic bandgaps in nanostructured materials has led to the development of innovative opto-acoustic devices called phoxonic crystals. In yet another example, tailorable Brillouin scattering was recently reported in nano scal silicon waveguides.
声光在微小波导中复杂有趣的相互作用最近再次引发关注，主要得益于他们在现代物理关键领域的实验中被成功实现。举例来说，微纳结构的光子晶体光纤可以允许对光子和声子的紧束缚，从而使得其中布里渊散射的基本特性区别于标准光纤。包括小芯微结构光纤中多种较高频率横向和纵向混合声波的约束。最近光学微腔中声光强耦合现象也得到报道，同时引入了新的概念，入腔光机械或表面光机械、布里渊冷却、片上布里渊散射和微腔激光器。另外，最新的研究展示了微结构材料中可以同时实现声学带隙和光学带隙，从而导致名为phoxonic crystals(X学晶体)的新型声光器件的发展。在另一个例子中，报道了纳米尺度硅波导中可裁剪布里渊散射现象。

Among other microdevices, photonic silica microwires are the tiny as-yet underutilized cousins of optical fibers. These hair-like slivers of silica glass, fabricated by tapering optical fibres, enable enhanced nonlinear optical effects and applications not currently possible with comparatively bulky optical fibres. Kerr effect and stimulated Raman scattering for supercontinuum generation, Brillouin scattering(BS) in these tiny waveguides has not been explored yet. Until now, only transverse acoustic resonance have been reported in long optical fibre tapers with a large diameter of a few micrometres.
在其他的微器件中，光纤微米线是光纤微小但尚未得到充分利用的表亲。这些类似银色头发一样的二氧化硅玻璃线，通过拉锥普通光纤制备，可以提高光学非线性效应和相比较起来体块较大的普通光纤中所不能实现的应用，克尔效应和受激拉曼散射可以用于激发超连续谱，而微波导中布里渊散射尚未进行探索。到目前为止，只有长拉锥中的横向声学腔有过报道，其直径有数个微米之大。

In this work, we present what is to our knowledge the first complete measurement and numerical modelling of Brillouin scattering in a subwavelength-diameter optical fibres, revealing th full elastic wave distribution of such tiny optical wveguides. More specifically, we demonstrate the all-optical generation of a new class of surface acoustic waves(SAWs) and report the observation of SAW Brillouin scattering (SAWBS) in the bakward direction. In addition to surface waves, our experimental and theoretical investigations also show that silica microwires also exhibit several widely spaced Brillouin frequencies involving hybird shear and LAWs, as previously demonstrated in small-core PCFs. Our experimental measurements are checked against numerical simulations of the equations of elastodynamics including the electrostrictive stress.
本工作中，我们首次展示了亚微米光纤中布里渊散射的测量以及数值建模，揭示了这种微波导中完整的弹性波分布情况。特别是，我们演示了全光激发的新型表面声波并报道表面声波的反向布里渊散射。除了表面波之外，我们的实验和理论研究同时解释了二氧化硅微米线也表现出一些宽间距的布里渊频率，涉及到剪切和表面波的混合，这在小芯的光子晶体光纤中有报道。我们的实验结果利用弹性动力学和电致伸缩力的公式加以数值模拟验证。

Results
Principle. Let us first describe the opto-mechanical interaction under consideration. When coherent laser light is coupled and guided into a long uniform optical microwire as that shown schematically in Fig. 1, the light both excites and feels several types of elastic waves with similar micrometre-scale wavelengths. In standard optical fibres, the light is guided into the core and is therefore solely sensitive to both shear and longitudinal bulk acoustic waves, leading to well-known physical effects such as guided scoustic wave Brillouin scattering and stimulatd Brillouin scattering (SBS), respectively. In contrast, in subwavelength-diameter optical fibres, the guided light and particularl the evanescent field interact with the outr surface. Light can thus shake the wire and generate SAWs. The associated effective refractive index along the optical microwire. When passing through this moving refractive index grating, light undergoes Bragg scattering in the backward direction according to the phase-matching condition, as in fibre-based Brillouin scattering form LAWs. The backscattered Brillouin signal also undergoes a slight shift of its carrier frequency due to the Doppler effect according to the photon-phonon energy conservation law vB=2n_eff_V/lammda, with neff, the effective refractive index of the microwire; lammda, the optical wavelength in vacuum and V, the acoustic phase velocity. The acoustic velocity however significantly differs for surface, shear and longitudinal waves. Surface waves travel at a velocity between 0.87 and 0.95 of a shear wave (for fused silica, V_s=3,400ms^-1). This gives rise to new optical sidebands down-shifted from 6GHz in the light spectrum(FIG. 1c). On top of surface waves, there are also bulk hybrid acoustic waves (HAW) involving both shear and longitudinal components because of the mehanical boundary conditions(Fig. 1b). HAWs propagate at an intermediate speed between shear and longitudinal waves with acoustic frequencied ~9GHz. Furthermore, the sound energy density of surface waves is confined at the air-silica interface of the microwire, leading to small mechanical ripples of a few picometre, whereas, the HAW energy density remains trapped within the core without altering the wire shape(Fig. 1c).
结果
原理。让我们首先考虑描述光-机相互作用。如图1所示，当相干激光耦合并导入长的均匀光纤微米线中，激光同时激发并感受几种不同的弹性波，其波长在几微米左右，同激光波长大小类似。在标准光纤中，激光被导入芯层，因此只对剪切和纵向体波敏感，从而导致常见的物理效应如声学导波布里渊散射和受激布里渊散射。相反，在亚波长直径微光纤中，导光和倏逝波同光纤外表面相互作用。因此光可以振动波导从而产生表面波。与之相联系的机械波纹导致光纤微米线上有效折射率微小的周期性变化。当激光通过这些移动的折射率光栅，根据相位匹配条件，就会产生反向的布里渊散射，这一过程同纵波布里渊散射类似。反向散射的布里渊信号的载波频率会有一个多普勒平移，这是由于光子-声子的能量守恒规律所决定：VB=2n_eff_V/lammda，其中neff是微光纤的有效折射率，lammda是光真空波长，V是声波相速度。表面波剪切波和纵波的声速有显著的不同。表面波的传播速度大概是剪切波的0.87到0.95倍(对于熔融石英，V_s=3,4000ms^-1)。这就会引起光学频带6GHz的下降，图1c显示了波长移动图。在表面波频率之上，还会存在混合体声波(HAW)，包括剪切和纵波成分，这主要是由于机械边界条件决定，见图1b。混合波的传播速度为剪切波和纵波中间值，其声波频率为~9GHz。另外，表面声波的能量密度限制在空气和二氧化硅的界面出，使得机械波纹大概皮米量级，而体波的能量密度限制在芯层从而不会改变微光纤的形态。
